Introduction

Making cupcakes is a fun and easy way to indulge in a sweet treat. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced baker, these cupcakes will be a hit at any party or event!

Recipe

Follow these simple steps to make your own cupcakes:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Pour the batter into cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  5. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let the cupcakes cool before frosting!
